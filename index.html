Watch your tax savings tick up as you drive

Set your federal bracket once. Then every mile shows up as a real dollar value in real time — not just a deduction line, but the actual tax saved at your bracket. The Dashboard ticker turns "I drove 14 miles" into "I just saved $3.24."

Tap. Drive. Save.

Tap Start, drive, tap End. Milely captures your route on a live map, calculates the miles, and asks you to classify the trip when you're done.

Or fully automatic

Opt into auto-detect and Milely uses motion + GPS to recognize when you start driving — no manual taps.

Audit Lock

January 31 each year, last year's records lock automatically. Unlocking is logged. Your CPA stops worrying about mid-audit edits, and you have a clean paper trail if the IRS ever asks.

Annual report, one tap

One-tap PDF reports with cover page, by-business breakdown, and per-trip details. Hand to your CPA.

Receipt scanner

VisionKit-powered scanner with edge detection, perspective correction, and multi-page support — same engine as Apple Notes.

Zero data collection

No analytics. No third-party SDKs. No Milely servers. Your data stays on your iPhone — period.

Backups your way

Automatic to iCloud, Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive. Or manual JSON export. Your data, your cloud.
