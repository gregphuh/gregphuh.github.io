  • + Receipts. VisionKit document scanner (edge detection, perspective correction, multi-page) and camera-roll attach. Receipts surface beside each trip and on the PDF report.
  • + Branded PDF reports. Your business logo on every cover, accent colors auto-matched to your brand, your firm name + contact info in the footer of every page.
  • + Five extra themes. Light, Forest, Mint, Sky, Slate — plus Brand mode that paints the app from any business logo.
  • + Automatic cloud backup. Hands-off backup to iCloud Drive or any Files-app cloud (Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive). One-tap restore on a new iPhone.
  • + Branded map route. Trip route renders in Milely's signature gradient instead of Apple's default blue.
  • + Priority email support. Front-of-the-line at milely@smileycreative.io.
  • + All future Plus features, automatic. While your subscription is active, every new Milely+ feature added in updates is yours — no extra purchases. (The base app does not receive new features.)

Auto-renews annually unless cancelled at least 24 hours before period-end. Cancel any time in Apple's subscription management. Apple handles all billing.
